📜 The Legend of Hmglish

Once upon a conversation, a husband poured his heart out in a long, sweet message... and all he got back was: “hm”. 💔

But instead of feeling ignored, he had a genius idea — what if “hm” is actually a secret code? Thus, Hmglish was born: a binary-style encoding language where H = 0 and M = 1.

Now, you too can speak in pure "hm" and hide your deepest feelings in plain sight. 🧙‍♂️
